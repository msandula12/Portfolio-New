About

My name is Michael Sandula. I'm 27 years old. I graduated from Oakland University in 2010 with a bachelor's degree in journalism. Since then, I've worked as a copy editor for a few places, most recently The Oakland Press. In February and March of 2016, I attended the Grand Circus JavaScript Bootcamp. When I'm not knee-deep in JavaScript, I can be found behind my drumset or buried in a book.
